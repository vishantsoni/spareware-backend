WHAT IS MYORDERSLIP ?

An intelligently developed order management software for all the B2B owners (manufacturers, wholesalers, distributors, retailers). now nobody have to maintain those handwritten order receipts and customer details and waste their valuable time. this software can easily maintain order histories and manage them on just a click and enhance the productivity of your business.

ORDER MANAGEMENT

  • track and manage all the orders with range of filters
  • receive quick order notifications on your mobile anytime, anywhere.
  • partial orders got automatically generated.

DYNAMIC CATALOGS

  • create as many beautiful catalogs of products as you want.
  • apply discount catalog wise and assign them to the specific customers.

WHOLESALA PRICING AND PACKAGING

  • sell your products by per unit, packaging unit and master packaging unit wise.
  • set the price of per unit and our intelligent system will auto calculate the wholesale pricing and show your customers.

MANAGE INVENTORY

  • receive orders according to your instock inventory.
  • no end time cancellations due to nill stocks.

MANAGE STAFF

  • assign them system modules accordingly.
